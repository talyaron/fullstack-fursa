Ar'ara (Arabic: عرعرة, Hebrew: עַרְעָרָה; lit. "Juniper tree")[2] is an Arab town in the Wadi Ara region in northern Israel. It is located south of Umm al-Fahm just northwest of the Green Line, and is part of the Triangle. In 2019, the population was 25,245.[1]
